## Act 2 thesis

> Mark stops trying to survive 1976 and starts *living in it.* He falls for Iwona. He builds the machine. And then — at the moment of completion — everything breaks.

The emotional arc: **hate → love → must-leave.** Act 2 is the **hate → love** half.

## Iwona arc (the love story)  📝 DRAFT

### Iwona introduction (early Act 2)

Iwona Kowalski just arrived on the block — Polish immigrant. Her father is a cargo-ship captain working out of Old Bay Harbor; her family is living with her uncle Jack Kowalski. **She can never go back to Poland** (Cold War / political defection logic).

Iwona is **Tier 0** — outside the Danny-knowledge tier system entirely. She never knew Danny. She only ever knows Mark-as-Danny. **She is the only person on the block where Mark doesn't have to perform cover** — there's no "prior Danny" to deviate from.

### The amber necklace (planted Act 2, paid off Act 3)

Iwona wears an amber necklace with an insect inclusion. **Plant it visually early.** No dialogue about it yet. Reason it's special: she bought it herself in Poland with hard-saved money before she came over. It reminds her of the home she can't return to.

### The beaches banter (planted Act 2)

Recurring joke between Mark and Iwona: comparing American beaches (amusement parks, arcades, boardwalks, big waves) to Polish beaches (cold Baltic, dunes, forests, **amber on the shoreline**). The amber reference must land in Act 2 as a throwaway joke. **Player doesn't yet know amber matters.**

## All-Hope-Is-Lost moment (Act 2 ending)  🔒 LOCKED

**The machine test-fires. It fails. Worse than the first time.**

- The Tear **rips visibly open.**
- The 2026 bedroom **bleeds through the walls** of Danny's 1976 room.
- PAL is **screaming.**
- The 1976 room is **on fire.**
- The machine is **dead.**
- Mark sees Iwona's necklace through the fire haze.

Then comms: Danny's voice cuts through the chaos —

> *"I'm scared. I want to come home. Please."*

**Player choice — playable leap of faith.** Press the swap switch with no idea what will happen.

The player presses it. **End of Act 2.**
