# Ending — Back to 2027

> **Status:** 🔒 CANON — beat structure · 2026-05-30. Canonical *structure* for this beat. The fully-scripted Lamborghini scene + post-credits stinger live in [`../story-arc.draft.md`](../story-arc.draft.md) (script layer), pending scene-by-scene fold.

**Length:** Short. BTTF-style closer. Sequel hook implied, not stated.
**Tone:** Bittersweet → comedy beat → curiosity hook.

---

## Beat 1 — Mark wakes up in 2027  📝 DRAFT

Mark opens his eyes. His own bedroom. **The screens are quiet.**

(Why quiet? PAL was screaming through the Tear during the Act 2 All-Hope-Is-Lost moment. Maybe PAL is gone. Maybe 2027 has changed. Player is uncertain.)

❓ **Open question:** What does Mark see *first* that confirms he's back? The hologram screens? An object from 2027? Or — and this is the better instinct — **does he immediately notice something is different about 2027?**

---

## Beat 2 — Danny is here  🔒 LOCKED ENERGY

Danny is in 2027. Old. **~65 years old.** He's been here a long time — except he hasn't; he's been here *since the swap fired*, which from Mark's POV was minutes ago. From Danny's POV: **50 years.**

(This is the back-to-the-future handwave that gives us our "Danny figured a lot of stuff out" budget — anything that doesn't quite track in this ending or a sequel is covered by *"Danny had 50 years to refine the machine."*)

### Danny's line (paraphrase, refine later)

> *"Get me out of here. This place sucks. I'm sick of being an old man."*

**This implies:**
- The world has changed in ways Mark doesn't know about.
- Danny has been working on something for 50 years.
- Whatever it is, it's not done from Danny's POV.
- **There is a sequel.**

---

## Beat 3 — The amber  📝 DRAFT

Mark feels the weight around his neck. **Iwona's amber necklace is here, in 2027.**

The amber **survived the swap.** It survives reversals — that's the rule.

❓ Does Danny see/comment on it? Does Mark just touch it silently? Does the player get a choice to show it / hide it?

(Lore cross-ref: This same necklace is what Danny will eventually use to bring Iwona forward into Sylvia's body. **Mark doesn't know this.** Player who has been paying attention may piece it together. Or it's pure plant for sequel.)

---

## Beat 4 — Iwona / Sylvia hint  ❓ OPEN

The biggest design question of the ending.

**Options on the table:**

- **(a) No hint.** Mark goes back to his life. The amber stays around his neck as a private reminder. Sequel hook is pure Danny ("get me out of here"). Iwona-Sylvia is a complete back-story handwave delivered in a one-liner.

- **(b) One-liner from Danny.** Danny says something like: *"Don't worry. She's coming."* Mark has no idea what he means. Sequel hook is overt.

- **(c) Show Sylvia.** Mark steps outside. Sees a girl who looks like Iwona's age, in 2027. **The eyes are Iwona's.** No dialogue. Cut to black.

**Clawpilot recommendation:** (a) or (c). (b) over-tells. Mark advocated for "we don't need to be perfect but we don't want any huge holes people are going to talk about on social media" — and the sequel hook is enough holes-coverage on its own.

## Cross-cutting things this ending MUST honor

- 🔒 **The amber survived.** Same necklace. Same insect inclusion.
- 🔒 **Danny is old.** The "50 years" handwave is in play.
- 🔒 **Mark doesn't know about future Iwona-Sylvia swap.** Player may suspect.
- 🔒 **Buster's grave** (where the binder was buried) — does Mark visit it post-swap? Does he need to? (Currently: binder is buried because cemetery persists; this is a future-game mechanic if needed.)

## What this ending refuses to do

- ❌ No long exposition dump explaining the time-loop mechanics.
- ❌ No on-screen Iwona-Sylvia swap (that's the sequel's job).
- ❌ No tearful farewell phone call to 1976 Iwona (the kiss WAS the goodbye).
- ❌ No "and they all lived happily ever after" — Danny is unhappy in 2027, that's the comedy.
