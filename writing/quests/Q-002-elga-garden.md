## 3. What Player KNOWS (Initial Signposts)

Plant during Q-001 and ambient world:

| Signpost | Location / Trigger | Player-type targeted |
|---|---|---|
| Q-001 close-out: Elga mentions "the garden is a terror" | End of Q-001 dialogue | All players |

**The "a-ha" trigger:** the word **funny-smelling** in Elga's pitch combined with the visible plant shape. That's the chain.

---

## 4. What Player FIGURES OUT (The Puzzle)

> *"Wait — funny-smelling cigarettes + their seeds landed in her yard + grew into 'weeds'... Elga's weeds are MARIJUANA. And she has no idea."*

Logical chain:

1. Elga hires me for yardwork (surface goal: money)
2. She complains about the teenagers next door tossing "funny-smelling" butts over the fence
3. The "weeds" she wants pulled have five-pointed sticky leaves
4. The butts are hand-rolled, not Marlboros
5. Roaches (the ends of joints) often contain seeds
6. The seeds took root → marijuana plants
7. Elga is too out-of-it to know
8. I just pull the "weeds" and pocket them. Free Vapor brew ingredient.

**Casual player path:** does the yardwork, gets paid, weed auto-added to inventory, discovers it later at the brew step. No failure.

## 5. Failure Feedback (Diegetic, Not Punishing)

This quest is a labor mini-game with no "wrong answer" — but discovery rewards observation.

| Wrong attempt / Action | Response | Hint embedded |
|---|---|---|
| Don't examine anything during cleanup | Cleanup completes, weed silently added to inventory, completion cutscene plays | Player gets reward without realization (late-game a-ha) |
| Examine a weed (1st time) | "These don't look like dandelions. Five-pointed leaves." | Mild observation |
| Examine a weed (2nd time, different plant) | "Sticky. Smells… resinous." | Building |
| Examine a weed (3rd time) | Mark pauses. *"…Oh."* | Forced verbalization — player gets it |
| Examine a discarded butt | "Hand-rolled. Not a Marlboro. The end's all chewed up — a 'roach' — and there's a seed in it." | Hard hint |
| Approach the teenagers next door WITH a weed in inventory | One of them, eyes wide: *"Yo. Where'd you get THAT?"* | Hard confirmation |
| Show Denise a pulled weed (optional path) | Denise's response respects her spiritual-elder role: *"That's not something a boy should be carrying around. Throw it in the river. Or… don't. I'm not your mother."* — confirms what it is without endorsing | Spiritual-elder respect maintained; she knows but isn't the source |
