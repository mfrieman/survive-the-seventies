# World Bible — The Parody Universe

> **Core principle:** Every real-world brand, band, movie, TV show,
> product, food, and franchise that appears in *Survive the 70s* gets a
> fake in-game equivalent. F is for Family style. No real trademarks
> appear in the game.
>
> **Why:** (1) Zero copyright risk. (2) Every parody name is itself a
> joke. (3) It turns "70s nostalgia simulator" into **our own comedy
> world** that we own and can build out.

## Naming rules
1. **Sounds period-plausible.** "Burger Czar" works in 1979. "Burger
   Lord" sounds like a 2010s craft chain.
2. **Slightly dumb / on the nose.** Real 70s branding *was* dumb. Lean
   in.
3. **One-word or two-word max.** Long names die in dialogue.
4. **Far enough from the real thing legally.** If a lawyer would worry,
   change one more letter.
5. **The narrator never lampshades the parody.** He says "Super Star
   Adventures" with the same straight face the kids would. The
   audience gets it.

## The setting
**Region:** Fictional **Old Bay County, MD** — a Baltimore-shaped fake
county between Baltimore County and Anne Arundel. Baltimore equivalents
exist (Crab Cove for the Inner Harbor, etc.) but everything is renamed.
**Why "Old Bay":** the seasoning is peak Baltimore, the pun lands
instantly, and it tells you everything about the tone in two words.
Geography is compressed — places that were a real-life 90-minute drive
can be a short ride in the game.

See `world-map.md` for the full geography (the three zones, the
destination places like Ocean Side and Storybook Hollow, the
boardwalk shops, etc.).

## How to use this list
- When a real brand would naturally come up in dialogue, **swap to the
  fake**. Don't apologize, don't explain — the kid says "I want a Brain
  Freeze and a pack of Cracklin' Stones" and we move on.
- Don't reach for parodies that aren't on this list mid-scene; **add
  them here first**. This file is the canonical source.
- The parody version usually has **one extra dumb detail** the real
  thing doesn't (FizzCo is "the cola the astronauts drank," Buckmeister
  beer has a buck on the can wearing sunglasses, etc.). Sprinkle these
  in for color.
- **Logos and visual designs will need fake versions too** when we get
  to art. The Super Star Adventures bedsheet, the BLAZE patch on the
  back of a denim jacket, etc.
